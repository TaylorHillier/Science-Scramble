How to Play

First step: Choose your username! The game will not start unless you have entered one.


Difficulties

Each difficulty will change the word pool. Normal mode consists of mostly 4-7 letter words. Hard mode consists of mostly 6-10 letter words. You can play both difficulties for each game mode.


Game Modes

Frenzy: You have 2 minutes to get the most points possible. You have 10 seconds to answer each scramble. If you guess wrong or do not guess within 10 seconds, you lose a life. You have 3 lives at the beginning of the game. You cannot lose more than one life per word.


Classic: Continue playing through up to 50 rounds where the score you recieve is exponentially worse per second you take to answer. No lives on this gamemode in turn. You have 20 seconds to answer each scramble. If you let the timer run out, you lose. You can guess as many times as needed per turn.
